# Exthera-School LP Image2 prompt set

Mode: built-in image generation tool

Use case: `photorealistic-natural`

Shared production direction:

- Wide 3:2 two-panel diptych for paired landing-page case visuals.
- Bright contemporary outpatient rehabilitation clinic in Japan.
- Early-career Japanese physical therapist in plain deep navy scrubs.
- Left panel shows a realistic moment of uncertainty; right panel shows the same therapist and patient after gaining a clear clinical reasoning framework.
- Premium editorial documentary photography, natural skin texture, realistic anatomy and equipment, soft daylight.
- Cool white, deep navy and restrained cobalt-blue accents.
- No text, letters, numbers, logos, watermark, anime, illustration, cartoon, 3D render, floating icons or speech bubbles.

## Case 01: assessment to next step

Left: the therapist is uncertain after an assessment, holding a clipboard and considering which exercise to choose. Right: the same therapist confidently explains three appropriate exercise options to the patient using simple gestures and a resistance band.

## Case 02: template to individualization

Left: the therapist relies on a familiar generic exercise routine and looks unsure. Right: the same therapist tailors a different exercise to the patient's movement and daily goal.

## Case 03: pain to safe adaptation

Left: the patient pauses and touches her knee while the therapist looks momentarily unsure but calm. Right: the therapist safely adjusts the exercise to a supported lower-load variation and reassures the patient.

## Case 04: vague dosage to clear dosage

Left: the therapist hesitates over resistance and repetitions while holding a clipboard. Right: the therapist sets an appropriate resistance-band level and monitors form.

## Case 05: stalled follow-up to reassessment

Left: the therapist reviews the patient two weeks later and is uncertain whether to continue. Right: the therapist reassesses a functional movement, discusses progress and chooses the next progression.

## Case 06: template to shared reasoning

Left: the therapist defaults to a template while glancing between a generic exercise setup and the patient. Right: the therapist explains an individualized plan to the patient while two colleagues listen during a brief case discussion.
